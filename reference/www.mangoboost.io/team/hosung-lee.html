Hosung Lee

Business Management Team, Seoul

Ho Sung Lee joined MangoBoost in 2023 as a Strategy & Operations Manager. Before joining, he worked as an IT consultant of KT Corporation, delivering innovative and effective IT solutions to corporates and government organizations.

We're all about fostering an environment where everyone has the opportunity to learn, develop, and reach theirfull potential.

Why did you decide to join MangoBoost?

I have wanted to work in a deep-tech industry where we can provide state-of-the-art technology and create a huge impact in the market. MangoBoost has a high reputation for a DPU solutions provider and the opportunity to work with talented teammates and create business strategy in this fast-developing industry was a huge motivation for me to join MangoBoost.

How is it working at Business Management Team?

Our team is responsible with a wide variety of tasks related to both operation and strategy of our company. Everyday, I am learning valuable lessons and its exciting to be in charge of important tasks, from making strategic decisions to navigating complex business environments. Also, our teammates always help each other to tackle challenges together and it is a pleasure to work in an honest and open environment.

What are the opportunities for career growth at MangoBoost?

In MangoBoost, you can closely cooperate with industry veterans across technology and talented engineers to find creative ways to implement our solutions. Its a challenging and also a rewarding experience to analyze various aspects of fast-changing IT industry and decide our optimum business strategy. Im confident that it is a best workplace to hone business skills and maximize your potential.

What makes MangoBoost special?

We're committed to delivering top-notch results, pushing boundaries, and striving for nothing short of greatness in every project we tackle. But here's the kicker: we don't just stop at excellence. We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards, always doing what's right, even when nobody's watching. Also, we're all about fostering an environment where everyone has the opportunity to learn, develop, and reach their full potential. Whether it's through mentorship or simply encouraging each other to step out of our comfort zones, we're dedicated to helping each team member thrive and succeed.

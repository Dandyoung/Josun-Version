Fast Facts

Empower Scalable, High-Performance Disaggregated Storage with Ease

Mango StorageBoost™ - NVMe over TCP Target revolutionizes disaggregated storage systems, delivering near-local access speeds with unmatched performance. By leveraging the Ethernet-based TCP/IP protocol, it enables effortless scale-out and seamless integration into existing infrastructures.
